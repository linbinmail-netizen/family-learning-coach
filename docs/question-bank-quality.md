# Question Bank Quality Standard

This project should expand the question bank by quality first, quantity second.

## Quality Gate

Every production question should include:

- Clear grade and subject alignment, preferably TEKS/STAAR aligned.
- One focused skill tag so reports can identify weak knowledge points.
- A visible difficulty level: basic, medium, advanced, or challenge.
- Four distinct answer options with exactly one correct answer.
- Plausible distractors, not joke choices or obviously impossible answers.
- A short explanation that teaches the concept, not just the final answer.
- At least two coach hints that help the student reason step by step.

## Coverage Target

Priority subjects currently require:

- At least 8 local questions per subject.
- At least 1 advanced or challenge question per subject.
- Average quality score of 80 or above.

Priority subjects:

- Grade 8 Math
- Grade 8 RLA
- Grade 8 Science
- English I
- Algebra I
- Geometry
- Biology

## Expansion Rules

Do not copy school, STAAR, or commercial-platform questions verbatim. Use official standards and released item styles as reference, then write original questions.

When adding questions:

- Mix formats across calculation, reasoning, data interpretation, and explanation.
- Avoid answer-position patterns.
- Make wrong choices represent common student misunderstandings.
- Add coach hints before adding large batches of new items.
- Run the automated quality audit before release.

## Version Roadmap

- v4.0: Add rubric, coverage targets, and automated audit functions.
- v4.1: Show quality dashboard and weak-subject gaps in the app.
- v4.2: Expand high-priority weak areas with higher-difficulty items.
